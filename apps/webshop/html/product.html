Condition Report

Grade: Near Fine

Exterior

The original burgundy cloth shows minor rubbing to the head and tail of the spine. Corners are remarkably sharp with only negligible softening. The gilt lettering remains bright and untarnished, retaining 95% of its original luster.

Interior

Endpapers are original and show characteristic but light foxing. The text block is remarkably clean, free from marginalia or previous owner signatures. Hinges are firm and have not been reinforced.

History & Provenance

This specific volume was once part of the renowned Throckmorton Estate Library in Oxfordshire. It was acquired by the current owner in 1982 via Sotheby’s London.

Curatorial research suggests this printing was among the first batch of the 1884 reissue, noted for the slightly thicker paper weight used before the publisher shifted to a more economical stock later that year.
